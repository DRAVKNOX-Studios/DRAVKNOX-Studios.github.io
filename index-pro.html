About the studio

"Built by someone who refused to pick just one discipline, or put up with software that forgets who it's for."

Dravknox Studios is the umbrella for three distinct creative operations. Talvrek handles the tech: FOSS apps and tools that replace the bloated, subscription-locked garbage you've been tolerating. Embrvaal is where the games and 3D art live. Veltrun handles the sound.

All three run on the same conviction: software and games should be built for the people who actually use them. Optimized for average hardware. No strings attached. No enshittification roadmap.
